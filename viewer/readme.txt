Viewer controls
===============

Drag view (all views) - Left click
Center perspective view on point - ALT + left click
Select chart (perspective, texture view) - Right click
Zoom - Mouse wheel

Toggle wireframe on detail view - w

Highlight next greedy step - SPACE
Perform next greedy step - ENTER

Perform close operation on parameterization graph (tries to merge regions with only one adjacency) - c
